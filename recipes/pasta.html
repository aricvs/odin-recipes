Pasta

Description

A basic pasta with cherry tomatoes

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in water
  2. Fry the garlic in the olive oil
  3. Fry the cherry tomatoes with the garlic once the garlic is brown
  4. Mix the pasta in the frying pan with the other ingredients
