Lasagna

Description

A quick and easy lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Put a lasagna sheet on the bottom of a baking tray
  3. Put ground beef on top of the sheet
  4. Put the cheese on top of the beef
  5. Put the sauce on top of the cheese
  6. Repeat from step 2 to 5 until you run out of material, assembling everything into the tray
  7. Put it on the oven at 200 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes
