Steak

Description

A basic rib-eye steak

Ingredients

  • Rib-eye steak
  • Parilla salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up the frying pan with butter
  2. Add the steak
  3. Flip once the bottom is brown
  4. Cook until preferred doneness is reached
  5. Slice the steak and add the parilla salt
  6. Mix up the pieces with the salt
